01 — About Me

Who I Am

I'm a DevOps Engineer in the making — obsessed with building the invisible infrastructure that keeps software alive, fast, and reliable. I made a deliberate shift toward DevOps because I believe the real power lies not just in writing code, but in shipping it flawlessly, repeatedly, and at scale.

I work hands-on with Linux environments, cloud platforms like AWS, containerization with Docker, and CI/CD pipeline design. Every tool I learn gets tested in a real environment — VMs, WSL2, cloud instances, Kali Linux labs. Theory gets thrown away fast around here; it's all about what works.

I'm early in my career but moving fast. I approach every problem with a systems mindset — understanding how components interact, where bottlenecks form, and how automation eliminates human error. That's the DevOps philosophy, and I live it.

Currently targeting the AWS DevOps Engineer Professional certification while building real-world projects to back it up. I'm open to internships, junior roles, and collaborations where I can contribute and grow simultaneously.
